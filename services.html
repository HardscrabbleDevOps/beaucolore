Our Services

Let's talk about what Brand It can do for you today.

We offer


Corporate Wear

Brand It offers a wide selection of Promotional and Corporate Clothing to meet all your corporate wear needs. With our highly skilled tailors, we manufacture only the best quality that will set your brand apart from others. We also supply corporate clothing from large South African brands like Barron Clothing, Altitude and Amrod.


Graphic Design

Brand It has qualified and experienced graphic design experts who will work one on one with you to come up with only the best creative designs for your requirements. From company logos, company profiles, flyers, posters, brochures, calendars, social media and newspaper adverts, business cards, letterheads, complimentary slips, corporate signatures and Power Point presentation templates, booklets, billboards and so much more.


Printing

Brand It also offers high quality commercial printing services for all the above mentioned products and so much more. With our many years of experience in this industry as well as our highly trained and qualified printers, you can rest assured that with us you will receive only the best service and quality.


Corporate Gifting

Corporate gifts are an effective way to reward, thank and inspire employees, customers or potential customers, while still educating and informing people about your brand. Brand It offers a wide range of these including pens, keyholders, flash drives, mugs, bags, lanyards and more - all branded with your logo.


Signage and Vehicle Branding

Brand It also specialises in the design and production of signs and vehicle branding, including but not limited to banners and flags sign boards, digital posters, stickers and vinyl lettering pop-up displays, cut out letterng, lightboxes and display gazebos.


Consultancy

Brand It offers a wide range of consultancy services aimed at developing your brand. Many companies today do not have an existing marketing department and that is where we come in. From market research to the formulation of marketing strategies, we will work with your organisation as and when required. We have qualified Chartered Maketers (UK) who will work with you and ensure that your project or marketing campaign successfully meets your targeted goals.
